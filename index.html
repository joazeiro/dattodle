How to Play

An unknown Datto device model is picked by the system and your job is to figure out which one is it. Try as much as possible to not reference any Datto documentation, but if you need to, that's alright. Select the device you believe to be the correct one from the drop-down list.

There's four different tiles that can show up when you guess:


The answer is higher than your current guess.


The answer is lower than your current guess.


The answer doesn't match your current guess.


The answer matches your current guess.


Game ends when you guess the correct device or you use all six turns.
